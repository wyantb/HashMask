    About HashMask

    This browser extension is basically a wrapper around someone else's work - Chris Dary <umbrae@gmail.com>. So lots of credit to him.

    The extension part is us - a few guys from the Society of Software Engineers at RIT. If you have any problems, please contact Brian Wyant - main developer - at wyantb@gmail.com.

    Or just file a bug for us right here! Any notes of visual defects are welcome.

    HashMask lives on Github.

    And is released under the BSD license.

    Thanks for trying it out!
